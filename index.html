Simple Terms Explained

TermSimple Meaning
Agency FeeMoney you pay the marketing company for managing your ads.
Actual Ad SpendReal dollars Google charged for showing your ads.
ImpressionsHow many times your ad appeared on Google (even if no one clicked).
CPC (Cost Per Click)How much you paid Google every single time someone clicked your ad.
ClicksHow many people actually clicked your ad.
ConversionsHow many people took action (called, filled a form, booked, etc.).
CTR (Click-Through Rate)The % of people who saw your ad and clicked it. Higher = more compelling ad.
Conversion RateThe % of people who clicked your ad and then took action. Higher = your landing page is working.
Cost per ConversionHow much you spent on ads to get one conversion.
Blended CostThe real total cost after adding the agency fee.
